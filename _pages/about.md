I'M a ML Research Engineer studying how and why neural 
networks fail to generalize beyond their training 
distribution, with a focus on compositional 
reasoning and AI safety.

My current work benchmarks LSTM and Transformer 
architectures on controlled arithmetic tasks, 
investigating whether these models learn algorithmic 
reasoning or surface-level pattern memorization.

My background is in AI ethics, which shapes how 
I approach questions of model failure not just 
as an accuracy problem, but as a safety problem.
